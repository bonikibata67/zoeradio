ZOE RADIO is a free to air Radio station in Nairobi, Kenya seeking to impact the youth through media. Zoe is a Greek word meaning life, hence our Mantra; “Inspiring Lives”. Our goal is to empower the next generation with positive and uplifting messages that promote empowerment, resilience, and hope.
